Common Questions

Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your Apple ID at the top → Subscriptions → tap Kibo → tap Cancel Subscription. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.
Open Kibo → go to the Settings tab → scroll down to the About section → tap Restore Purchases. Your subscription will be re-linked to your Apple ID automatically.
XP is your permanent score and never resets. Earn 150 XP for every completed gym session and 25 XP for each custom habit you tick off. Hydration and sleep are tracked for your health but don't award XP.
Your streak increments by 1 for each day you complete a gym session. Missing a single day resets it to 0. Streaks reset at midnight local time, so make sure you log your session before the day ends.
Email kibohabits@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and include the email address linked to your Kibo account. We'll permanently delete all your data within 30 days.
Open the Settings app → Privacy & Security → Health → tap Kibo and make sure the data types you want to share are enabled. Then go to Kibo Settings → tap Connect Apple Health again.
