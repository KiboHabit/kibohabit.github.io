Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kibo ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use the Kibo app and related services. If you are under 13 years of age, you may not use Kibo.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Account & Profile: Name, username, email address, date of birth, sex, height, and weight — used to personalise your habit plan and daily targets.
  • Health & Activity Data: Habit logs, gym sessions, hydration tracking, sleep goals, streaks, and creature progression data you input into the app.
  • Profile Photos: Photos you upload for your profile picture, processed by our AI verification system.

Information collected automatically:

  • Apple Health Data: With your explicit permission, Kibo reads steps, sleep, and workout data from Apple Health to enhance your experience. This data is never used for advertising and is never shared with third parties.
  • Usage Data: App interactions and session data used to improve the product.

How We Use Your Information

  • To power your Kibo experience: streaks, habit logging, creature evolution, and personalised daily targets.
  • To authenticate your account securely via Google or Apple sign-in.
  • To manage your Kibo Pro subscription through RevenueCat.
  • To improve the app's AI-powered gym verification and personalisation features.
  • To respond to support requests and communicate important updates.

We never sell your data or share it with advertisers. Health data stays on your device and in your account — it is never used for any purpose beyond powering your Kibo experience.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database storage, and cloud functions. Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management and in-app purchase processing. Privacy Policy
  • Apple HealthKit: Optional integration — read-only, with your explicit permission.

Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. To request deletion of your account and all associated data, email us at the address below. We will process your request within 30 days.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under GDPR (EEA/UK) or CCPA (California) to access, correct, or delete your personal data, or to opt out of certain data processing. Contact us at any time to exercise these rights.

Business Transfers

If Kibo is acquired by or merged with another company, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify users of any such change and ensure your data continues to be protected under equivalent terms.
